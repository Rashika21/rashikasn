About Me

I am currently working as a Software Developer at Research and Innovation unit of Enginium, Italy. It is amusing to wake up everyday and get to work with brilliant minds who are upgrading the Spacecraft on-board systems and  Attitude & Dynamics control systems. 

I earned my Master degree in Space Engineering, at the Politecnico di Milano, Italy and my Bachelor's in Aerospace Engineering from Alliance University, India. 

October 22 of 2008 is a remarkable day for every Indian, the first mission to the moon (Chandrayaan-I) was launched by the Indian Space Research Organization. I wondered how these huge rockets did not fall from the sky. Curiosity and eagerness became the driving force to develop an interest in Space science and technology. My journey in Asia and Europe has been blissful with an enormous experiences, memories and adventures. I would like to work on sophisticated space missions by providing lean solutions. And I aspire to motivate youths in taking up STEM as majors. After all, I believe in the saying - "Life Is What You Make It".
